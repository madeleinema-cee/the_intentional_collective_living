Us in a few words


We are

A community-focused non-profit

Based in Ann Arbor, currently serving all of SE Michigan and NW Ohio

An open community

Of change agents, individuals and organizations dedicated to helping each other grow & thrive

A platform

Of programs, tools and services that help people and organizations grow, thrive and connect

We help individuals

Heal and grow

From and through major life transitions and challenges

Connect authentically

With people that have been through it and well-intentioned organizations that can help

Give back

Using personal stories, experiences, and resources to inspire and help others during challenging times

We help organizations

Better serve existing clients

By easily finding and tapping into the connections and resources in our community

Scale your impact

Find more clients to serve and add to the things you can do for them

Collaborate

Share resources with like-minded organizations to accelerate impact and help each other grow

We go back to the basics of being a connected community: our vulnerability, desire to love and be loved, desire to help, and the innate connection we have with others. We're a safe, supportive, nurturing, equitable and inspiring space to unfold in.
