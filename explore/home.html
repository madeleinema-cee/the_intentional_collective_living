We are a community that stands strong together during times of change

Together, let’s rediscover what it means to be a community, to be human: our vulnerability, desire to love and be loved, willingness to help, and the innate connection we have with one another.

Join our community Learn more about us ↓


In times of change or big life challenges we provide a safe and creative place to:

Find and be YOU

Wherever you are on your path, we honor you and we have the community and resources to meet you where you are.

Ground and connect

Find your safe port in the storm by connecting with others that “get you”. You don't have to figure it all out on your own.

Heal and grow

Learn from those that have been through it, while being supported by our community of service providers.

Give back

Share your story and/or resources to help others along their journey. A great way to pay it forward!
